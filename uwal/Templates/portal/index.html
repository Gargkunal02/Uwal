About Us

UWAL HEALTHCARE is one of the fastest growing and leading pharmaceutical company in India, which provides highest standard of quality products. We are into Manufacturing and Trading (Export and Domestic) of our high efficacy products across the globe. We have established world-class manufacturing facilities by leveraging state of the art technology, incorporating best practices and adhering to stringent regulatory compliance. We employ impeccable strategies which reflect our commitment and dedication towards our quality products to escalate the awareness about one of the most vital human agenda Health.

Vision
May All become Happy, May All be healthy (free from illness) May all see what is auspicious, may no one suffer in any way.

UWAL HEALTHCARE is a specialist pharmaceutical company which is involved in pursuing a multilayered growth strategy which incorporates internal products development, strategic collaborations and symbiotic acquisition of businesses and pharmaceutical products. Our focus is to make quality pharma products better by finding effective ways to deliver them with greater safety, purity, stability and effectiveness. We always define our success in human terms and thus are proud of our accomplishment to provide the society with 100% safe and economical pharmaceuticals and healthcare products.
